The Secret Weapon

The Adaptive Remediation Loop

When a provider answers incorrectly, the system doesn't just show the answer. It creates a mini-learning moment. This is the novel mechanic that no competitor has implemented.

1
Question presented
Adaptive difficulty based on running performance
2
Incorrect answer detected
Wrong answer highlighted, correct answer shown
3
Forced rationale reading
Full explanation: why correct answer is right, why each distractor is wrong, clinical relevance. Must read before continuing.
4
Follow-up verification question
A similar (not identical) question on the same concept. Tests whether the rationale was actually learned, not just read.
5
Continue exam
Data captured for diagnostic report. The learning happened at the moment of the mistake.
Why this works

Immediate corrective feedback + retrieval practice produces 15–25% better long-term retention than passive content review alone. Cross-profession precedent exists in pharmacy (ACPE), nursing (ANCC), and medicine (ACCME/ABEM). Zero EMS CE providers have implemented this.
