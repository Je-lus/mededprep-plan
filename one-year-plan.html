CE Differentiation: The Diagnostic-Gap-Closure Loop
No other CE provider in EMS does this. Recert (J&B/PSG) sells CE hours. MedEdPrep sells CE hours that also generate diagnostic data:
  1. Clinician takes adaptive assessment
  2. IRT engine identifies specific knowledge gaps
  3. System prescribes specific CAPCE-accredited CE module addressing that gap
  4. Clinician completes CE and retakes relevant section to verify gap is closed
  5. Clinician gets CE credit AND verified competency improvement
Price CE at a premium justified by the diagnostic component. Do not race Recert to the bottom on per-course price.

⚠ Risks & Mitigations 8 identified risks ▼
1. Jeramey Capacity Constraint HIGH
As sales grow, support and onboarding demands increase. Jeramey gets pulled from development into support. Product improvements slow. Competitive advantage erodes.
Mitigation: Build self-serve onboarding early (Q2). Create KB and FAQ. Heather/Samantha are first-line support for sales questions. Hire if support consistently exceeds 20 hrs/wk and dev stalls 2+ months (budget at $20K MRR).
2. Heather Underperforms MEDIUM
She has the network, but relationships may have cooled since leaving her state-level position. Conversion could be slower than projected.
Mitigation: Capped commission = limited fixed cost if she doesn't sell. Monitor demos-to-closes ratio monthly. If below 15% conversion after 90 days, diagnose messaging vs. approach. Conservative scenario still nearly matches 2025 revenue.
3. Competitor Response MEDIUM
Platinum improves their "CAT" marketing. Fisdap refreshes exams. Pearson acquires someone.
Mitigation: Move fast on CoAssist adoption and B2C growth. These are structural advantages hard to replicate. Every month gains more switching cost. Build the moat through data and relationships, not features alone. 12-18 month window.
4. CoAssist Without Conversion LOW-MED
Programs use CoAssist (free) but never convert to paid products. MedEdPrep bears infrastructure cost with no return.
Mitigation: Minimal infra cost (same servers). Even 10-15% conversion is valuable. Brand awareness from 100+ CoAssist users is worth it for competitive positioning alone. Track conversion starting Q3.
