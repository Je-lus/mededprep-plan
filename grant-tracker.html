Georgia Business Certificate of Exemption
10-year exemption from local occupation taxes, admin fees, and regulatory fees. Requires 10%+ service-connected disability rating (25% for peacetime). Complete VS Form 40-025, bring DD-214 and VA Summary of Benefits to County Probate Court.
Unlocks: 10 years exemption from local business taxes and regulatory fees

Open Now

Rolling pitch system resumed April 2026 post-reauthorization. Submit a 1,500-word pitch across 4 prompts. NSF reviews within 3-6 weeks and invites full proposal. No topic-specific solicitations — you pitch your innovation.
SBIR Phase I Rolling Submission Deep Tech
Application Process & Requirements
  • Step 1 — Project Pitch: ~1,500 words across 4 prompts (Technology Innovation, Technical Objectives/Challenges, Market Opportunity, Company/Team)

Georgia R&D Tax Credit
Ongoing
10% of Qualified R&D
10% state tax credit on qualified research expenses exceeding the base amount, plus federal Section 41 R&D credit. MedEdPrep's psychometric R&D, adaptive algorithm development, and IRT modeling likely qualify. Claim on Georgia income tax return.
Tax Credit No Separate Application State + Federal
Requirements & Details
  • Claim on Georgia income tax return with supporting documentation
  • Consult a CPA familiar with state R&D credits
  • Qualifying activities: psychometric R&D, adaptive algorithm development, IRT modeling, CAT engine work
  • Federal Section 41 credit is in addition to the Georgia credit

Also available: Retraining Tax Credit ($500/employee/year for retraining programs), Job Tax Credit (up to $4,000/job in less-developed counties)

MedEdPrep Angle
