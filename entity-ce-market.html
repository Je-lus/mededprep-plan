Regulatory Tailwind
As of 2023, NREMT permanently removed all limits on distributive (online) education. All 40-60 CE hours can now be completed 100% online. Some states (e.g., California) still require 50% live, but the trend is toward full online acceptance.

Free CE Assessment
In most states, a provider can cobble together ~10-20 free CAPCE hours from various sources. That's only 25-50% of requirements. Virginia is the one exception (free Vector Solutions via state contract). Georgia TRAIN exists but is low-quality, limited topic coverage, and poor UX. The real competitive threat is the $35-72/yr "good enough" tier, not free CE.
